### About TRISTAN

[TRISTAN](https://www.chips-ju.europa.eu) is the first in a series of
projects sponsored by the European [Chips Joint Undertaking (Chips
JU)](https://www.chips-ju.europa.eu) that specifically focuses on
RISC-V technologies. Its overarching goal is to expand, mature and
industrialize the European RISC-V ecosystem so that it is able to
compete with existing commercial alternatives.

### The thematic session

The following quick talks (10&nbsp;min. each) will illustrate the
contributions of TRISTAN to RISC-V:
- **Welcome and Introduction to TRISTAN** &mdash; Patrick Pype, NXP
  Semiconductors
- **The role of Open Source EDA tools in RISC-V** &mdash; Bernhard
  Fischer, Siemens
- **The Open Source Software landscape for RISC-V** &mdash; Wolfgang
  Ecker , Infineon
- **The Unified RISC-V repository** &mdash; Charley Mann, Eclipse
  Foundation
- **How to build a competitive RISC-V core (CVA6) in Open-Source**
  &mdash; Jean Roch Coulon, Thales
- **Key take-aways of TRISTAN** &mdash; Patrick Pype, NXP
  Semiconductors
- *And a number of demonstrator pitches!*
